About Sherwood Private School

At Sherwood Private School, we are committed to nurturing excellence from Nursery to Secondary. Our mission is to provide a safe, stimulating environment for academic and personal growth, shaping leaders of tomorrow.

Mission

To provide an exceptional learning environment where every child is empowered — academically, socially, and morally — to build character, cultivate curiosity, and reach their full potential.

Vision

To become a leading private school in Lagos, recognized for holistic education, nurturing confident, responsible and globally‑aware citizens.

Excellence

Striving for academic and personal excellence in all areas.

Creativity

Encouraging innovative thinking and artistic expression.

Integrity

Fostering honesty, respect, and responsibility in students.

Community

Building a supportive and inclusive environment for all.
